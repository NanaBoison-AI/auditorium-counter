# User Guide: Auditorium Counter

Welcome to Auditorium Counter! This guide will help you set up your venue, manage events, and track attendance in real-time.

As an Admin, you control the venue layout, schedule events, and monitor live counts.

### 1. Getting Started

* **Sign Up:** On the login screen, switch to the "Admin Login" tab. Click **"Need an account? Create one"** to register with your email and password.

* **Organization Setup:** 1.  Once logged in, click the **Shield Icon** üõ°Ô∏è in the top header.
  2.  Enter your **Organization Name** (e.g., "City Church" or "Main Hall").

  * *Note: Your staff will need to type this exact name to log in, so keep it simple.*

### 2. Creating Layout Templates

Before starting an event, define your venue's seating structure.

1. Click **"Create New Event"** from the main dashboard.

2. **Add Blocks:**

   * Enter a Name (e.g., "Balcony Left"), Number of Rows, and Seats per Row.

   * Click **Add**.

3. **Add Other Areas:**

   * For standing room or stage areas, use the "Define Other Areas" section.

4. **Save as Template:**

   * Click the **Templates** button (File Box icon).

   * Enter a name (e.g., "Standard Concert") and click **"Save New"**.

   * *Tip: You can load this template later to instantly set up future events.*

### 3. Running an Event

1. **Start:** In the Setup view (or after loading a template), click the **"Start Event"** button (Play icon).

2. **Details:**

   * **Event Name:** e.g., "Friday Night Jazz".

   * **Passcode:** Create a numeric code (e.g., "5555").

   * *Important: Share the Organization Name and Passcode with your ushers.*

3. **Monitor:** You will be taken to the **Monitoring Dashboard**. Watch as numbers update in real-time.

### 4. Managing Live Events

* **Locking:** Click the **Lock Icon** üîí to freeze counts. Staff cannot edit numbers while locked. Click again to unlock.

* **Ending:** When the event is over, click the **End Event** button (Power icon). This logs out all counters and archives the data.

## üë§ For Counter Staff

As a Counter, your job is to report attendance for your specific section.

### 1. Logging In

1. Open the app and ensure you are on the **"Counter Login"** tab.

2. Enter the **Organization Name** provided by your admin.

3. Enter the **Passcode** for the specific event.

4. Click **"Join Event"**.

### 2. Choosing Your Section

1. **Select Event:** If multiple events are active, tap the one you are working at.

2. **Select Block:** You will see a list of sections (e.g., "Front B1", "Balcony"). Tap the section assigned to you.

### 3. Counting

* **Enter Counts:** You will see a list of rows. Type the number of people seated in each row.

* **Capacity Warning:** The app knows how many seats are in a row. It will prevent you from entering a number higher than the row's capacity.

* **Totals:** The top of your screen shows the total count for your specific section and the grand total for the entire venue.

### 4. Troubleshooting

* **"Locked" Icon:** If you see a red lock icon, the Admin has paused counting. You cannot edit numbers until they unlock it.

* **Refresh:** If you accidentally refresh the page, don't worry! The app remembers where you were and will put you right back into your section.
